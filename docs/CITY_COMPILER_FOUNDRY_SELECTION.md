# City Compiler — Foundry selection

_Last updated: 2026-07-21_

## Decision

**Selected production candidate: `foundry-pilot-04`.**

The selection was made after comparing the highest-quality candidate with two structurally diverse alternatives generated by City Compiler 2.

## Why candidate 04

```text
Foundry score       96.9
Complete-city score 84.9
Baseline city score 82.7
Roof areas             4
Roof routes            3
```

Candidate 04 provides the strongest balance of:

- two readable vehicle chase loops;
- five industrial blocks with housing, loading-bay and machine-shop families;
- the most complete rooftop circuit among the reviewed alternatives;
- two independent street-to-roof entries;
- two sewer entrances;
- four body-hiding dumpsters;
- eight streetlights;
- one parked utility vehicle;
- stable semantic IDs shared by every generated seed;
- zero new hard-validation exceptions.

Candidate 16 was more purely industrial but lost one roof and one route. Candidate 15 remained close in score but also simplified the vertical network. Because vertical gameplay is the current city's weakest measured component, candidate 04 is the better production foundation.

## Locked identifier

The selected seed is exported from:

```text
tools/city-compiler/foundry-selection.js
```

```js
SELECTED_FOUNDRY_SEED = "foundry-pilot-04"
```

Tests verify that the selected seed remains hard-valid, exceeds the complete-city baseline, retains four generated roofs and three generated rooftop routes, and contains the Foundry utility vehicle.

## Integration boundary

The generator PR still does not change the playable map. The next integration PR must:

1. export candidate 04 into a runtime district module;
2. compose that module into the normal district data;
3. preserve the generator as the reproducible source;
4. expose the candidate through an isolated preview scenario first;
5. validate driving loops, pedestrians, rooftops, sewers, lights, dumpsters and the utility vehicle in Chromium;
6. only then replace the authored Foundry data in the normal playable city.
