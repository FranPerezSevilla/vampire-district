# Playtest checklist

Use this before sharing the prototype with a new tester.

## First-run clarity

The player should understand this in the first 30 seconds:

- You are a vampire responsible for the district.
- A journalist is about to expose the clan.
- The order is: find him, lure him, drain him, hide the evidence.
- Powers raise hunger.
- Witnesses, police and hunters are the pressure system.

## Smoke test runs

Try these before sending a build:

1. Finish the mission cleanly with Blood Sense, Whisper, elimination, body hide and safehouse return.
2. Reach the club without using Blood Sense first.
3. Use rooftops before and after the target is eliminated.
4. Break a lamp and a camera before the journalist is handled.
5. Let a witness run, then intercept them with E.
6. Leave the body visible and return toward the safehouse.
7. Open Help while moving, while feeding, and while a chase is active.
8. Open Feedback while moving, while feeding, and after the report.
9. Open Sound config and test mapped sounds.
10. Restart the run from the help panel if the tester feels stuck.

## Known prototype limits

- Audio beyond footsteps and sprint is intentionally muted until manually picked.
- Art is placeholder and systems-first.
- The current goal is one understandable mission, not a full sandbox.
- Best played with keyboard.

## What to ask testers

- Did you know what to do next?
- Did stealth feel fair or confusing?
- Did hunger feel like a useful cost?
- Did the body/evidence loop make sense?
- What was the first moment that felt fun?
- What was the first moment that felt unclear?
